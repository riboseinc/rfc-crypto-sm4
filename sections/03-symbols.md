# Symbols And Abbreviations

$$S xor T$$
: bitwise execlusive-or of two 32-bit vectors $$S$$ and $$T$$.
  $$S$$ and $$T$$ will always have the same length.

$$a <<< i$$
: 32-bit bitwise cyclic shift on $$a$$ with $$i$$ bits shifted left.
